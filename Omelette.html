Omelette

Description:

An omelette is a dish made from eggs, fried with butter or oil in a frying pan. It is a common practice for an omelette to include fillings such as chives, vegetables, mushrooms, meat, cheese, onions or some combination of the above.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Whisk 2 eggs with optional cream or milk.
  2. Melt butter over medium heat and add the egg mixture to the pan.
  3. Pan fry in butter until gold on the surface.
  4. Douse with any toping of your choice!
